Display

Choose what opens when you click the toolbar icon. The side panel stays open while you browse.

General

Accept 4 or 5 digits for all card types. Amex always allows 5 digits regardless of this setting.

Card Fields

Adds a CVV field to each saved card. Stored locally in your browser, synced via your browser account. Only enable this if you are the sole user of this browser profile.

Notes

Adds a Notes button to each card for up to 3 reward categories or spending tips.

How notes appear when you click the Notes button.

Security

Prompts for a PIN each time the extension opens. The PIN is stored locally in this browser only, not synced.

4 to 16 characters. Letters and digits allowed.

How long after unlocking before the PIN is required again.

PIN required once per day, after this time in the selected timezone.

Excluded Sites

Data

WhichCard stores your card labels and preferences in your browser only. No card numbers are stored, only SHA-256 hashes of the last 4 or 5 digits. Nothing is sent to any server. No analytics. No tracking pixels. No backend.
