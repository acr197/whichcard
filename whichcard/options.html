Card Fields

Adds a CVV field to each saved card. Stored locally in your browser, synced via your Google account. Only enable this if you are the sole user of this browser profile.

Notes

Adds a Notes button to each card for up to 3 reward categories or spending tips.

How notes appear when you click the Notes button.

Security

Prompts for a PIN each time the extension opens. PIN is stored locally in this browser only, not synced.

4–16 characters. Letters and digits allowed.

How long after unlocking before the PIN is required again.

PIN required once per day, after this time in the selected timezone.

Data & Privacy

WhichCard stores your card labels and preferences in your browser only. No card numbers are stored — only SHA-256 hashes of the last 4 or 5 digits. Nothing is sent to any server. No analytics. No tracking pixels. No backend.

Changes save automatically.
